Hey everyone,

Manually checking response sheets against the official answer key is a massive headache, so I built a free web app to automate the whole thing for NCET 2026.

Instead of scrolling through pages and doing the math yourself, you can just drop your response PDFs into the site and it calculates everything instantly.

Link to tool: https://ncet-2026-calculator.onrender.com/

How it works:

    Download all 8 of your response sheet PDFs from the official portal.

    Drag and drop them into the upload box on the site.

    Instantly get your total score, accuracy, and a breakdown of Correct/Incorrect/Unanswered/Dropped questions.

A few cool features I added:

    Interactive Matrix: There's a table at the bottom showing every question. If you want to see "what if" scenarios, you can change your given answer in the dropdown and your total score will recalculate dynamically in real-time!

    Handles Dropped Questions: The script automatically detects dropped questions and awards the +4 marks accordingly.

Privacy / Open Source: I don't save your PDFs or your scores. If you're a tech nerd and want to see how the PDF parsing works (or run it locally on your own machine), the whole project is open-source on my GitHub: https://github.com/Aaruxhhh/NCET-2026-Calculator/

Let me know if you guys find any bugs or have any suggestions. Hope this saves you all some time! Good luck with your results!
